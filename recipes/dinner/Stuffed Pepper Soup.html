Stuffed Pepper Soup

Active time: 35 minutes

Total time: 45 minutes

Serves

Stuffed pepper soup has lots of good stuff: bell peppers, ground beef, tomatoes, rice. All in one hearty bowl of soup!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups brown rice, precooked
  • 1 pound lean ground beef, precooked
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup onion, white diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 29 ounces tomatoes, diced
  • 14 ounces tomato sauce
  • 2 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano

Directions

  1. Cook the ground beef until brown (5-8 minutes). Drain the fat.
  2. Add peppers, onions, and garlic. Cook for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, broth, oregano, and salt + pepper. Cover and simmer on low for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve on top of 1/2 cup cooked brown rice.
