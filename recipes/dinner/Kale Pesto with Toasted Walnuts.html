Kale Pesto with Toasted Walnuts

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 10 minutes

Serves

Chock full of folate, this pesto will give you great sleep and lots of serotonin in the morning. Besides, being cheap, this pesto is super delicious on pasta or brushed on grilled chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups kale leaves, packed
  • 1 cup basil leaves, packed
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cups walnuts, chopped, toasted
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Directions

  1. In a food processor, combine kale, basil, and salt. Pulse 10-12 times or until the leaves are finely chopped.
  2. With the motor running, drizzle in olive oil. Scrape down the sides of the processor.
  3. Add the walnuts and garlic & process again. Then add the cheese.
  4. Toss with your favorite pasta and serve immediately.
