Baconator

Active time: 30 minutes

Total time: 30 minutes

Makes

Calories:

Per serving:

A (not necesarilly) healthier, homemade alternative to the processed fast food garbage version! The sandwich doesn't have any vegetables on it, so add romaine lettuce and tomato if you feel like it or you could eat a bowl of vegetable chips with this sandwich on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 bun, whole wheat hamburger
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tbsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tbsp paprika (or chili powder)
  • 2 slices of colby cheese
  • 6 strips of bacon
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the mayo and ketchup to make the signature sauce.
  2. Mix the garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika into the ground beef. Then form 1/4 pound patties.
  3. Toast the buns in the oven gently at 350Â°F. While this is happening, prepare the bacon to be fried.
  4. Fry the bacon until crispy and leave the bacon grease in the pan. Set the bacon aside to drip dry.
  5. Using the bacon grease, fry the burger patties until perfection. Use black pepper as needed at this stage; the bacon is usually naturally salty enough.
  6. Spread the signature sauce on the bottom of the top bun. Assemble the sandwich as such: bottom bun, cheese slice, burger, cheese slice, 3 slices of bacon, burger, 3 slices of bacon, top bun.
  7. Live happy.
