Bourbon Chicken

Active time: 25 minutes

Total time: 25 minutes

Serves

Very unhealthy, very delicious. Chinese, mall take out style. Just like mom used to not make.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs, boneless skinless, 1" pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup bourbon, (can be subbed with apple juice)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

    • For garnish

  • White rice, cooked
  • Green onions, sliced

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Season teh chicken with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Place the chicken in the pan and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side or until golden brown and fully cooked. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. While the chicken is cooking: in a medium bowl, whisk together bourbon, brown sugar, ketchup, apple cider vinegar, water, soy sauce, and cornstarch.
  4. Pour the mixture over the chicken and bring to a boil. Turn the heat down to low and simmer for 3-5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Serve over a bed of white rice with green onions.
