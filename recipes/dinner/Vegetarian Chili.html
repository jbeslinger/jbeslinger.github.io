Vegetarian Chili

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 45 minutes

Serves

This chili is jam packed with nutritious veggies; it's so good, you won't miss the meat.

Ingredients

    For chili

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 bell peppers, cored, red and green
  • 1 onion, yellow medium, diced
  • 2 carrots, medium, peeld & diced
  • 2 zucchinis, small, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 29 ounces tomatoes, diced
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 15 ounces vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups corn, frozen
  • 14 1/2 ounces dark red kidney beans, canned
  • 14 1/2 ounces black beans, canned

    • For garnish

  • Desired toppings - Cheese, avocado, red onion, sour cream, cilantro

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Add bell peppers, onion, and carrots, saute 3 minutes.
  2. Add garlic and saute 2 minutes longer. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, broth, chili powder, cumin, coriander, salt + pepper. Cayenne pepper optional for spicy.
  3. Bring mixture to light boil, then reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in zucchini, simmer 10 minutes longer. Add corn, kidney beans, and black beans. Simmer for 2 more minutes.
  5. Thin with more broth to your liking, up to 1 cup and serve with toppings of choice.
