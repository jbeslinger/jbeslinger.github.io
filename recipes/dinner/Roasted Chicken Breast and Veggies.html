Roasted Chicken Breast and Veggies

Active time: 20 minutes

Total time: 60 minutes

Serves

A hearty and full dinner meal that encompasses veg, grain, and protein. Low on fat, filling, and delicious. Also very easy to make.

Ingredients

    For the chicken

  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 lemon
  • 10 ounces olive oil

    • For the vegetables

  • 5 ounces baby carrots
  • 1 onion, yellow diced
  • 1 head broccoli, chopped
  • 1 head cauliflower, chopped

    • For dessert

  • 1 slice zucchini bread

    • For garnish

  • Grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Pat the chicken breast dry & cut into 1/2" cubes.
  2. In a bowl, combine the spices, olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, and chicken breast. Toss everything. Cover and place in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 425Â°. Chop carrots and onion coursely, toss in the same spice/oil mixture, and bake in sheet pan for 20 minutes.
  4. Prep the broccoli and cauliflower and toss in spice/oil mixture.
  5. Take the chicken out of the fridge & the sheet out of the oven. Place everything into the pan on top of the carrots/onions evenly. Bake for another 15-20 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and top with parmesan cheese.
