Salmon Shawarma

Active time: 45 minutes

Total time: 2 hours 45 minutes

Serves

An attempt to make shawarma from cabinet goodies. We'll see how well this goes.

Ingredients

    For the salmon

  • 8 pieces thawed salmon filets
  • 2 cups plain greek yogurt
  • 1 onion, yellow, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried mint
  • Ground kosher salt to taste
  • Fresh ground black pepper to taste

    • For the yogurt sauce

  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon tahini (optional)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 5 leafs mint (optional)
  • Ground kosher salt to taste

Directions

    For salmon

  1. Mix all ingredients (minus the salt) in a large bowl and set in fridge for a couple hours to overnight.
  2. When it's ready, preheat oven to 400Â° and pour mixture in baking dish. Spread to about 1" thickness.
  3. Bake for 45 minutes or so, aiming to dry out the mixture slightly.
  4. Once the mixture comes together, sprinkle generously with salt and bake until it's dried up to your liking.

    5. For yogurt sauce

  5. Blend in a food processor until light and fluffy. Store up to 2 weeks.

    Serving suggestions

  • Stuff into pita bread with romaine salad mixture. Optionally, you can quarter cherry tomatoes & slice cucumber and place those in the wrap with it. Drizzle the yogurt sauce over the salad.
