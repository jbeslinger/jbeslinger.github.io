Red Pepper Pesto

Active time: 15 minutes

Total time: 15 minutes

Serves

A very healthy, red version of pesto that agrees with IBS without passing on any of the stuff that makes pesto taste great.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 3 peppers, red bell, roasted, roughly chopped
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup basil leaves, packed
  • 1/4 cup lactose-free cream (Or one part almond milk + one part canola oil)

    • To serve

  • Pasta

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta like normal. In a separate, dry pan, toast the pine nuts for a few minutes.
  2. Add the pine nuts to a food processor along with the red peppers, cheese, oil, basil, and salt + pepper to taste. Whizz to a paste, adding a splash of starchy, pasta water if it's a little dry.
  3. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the water. Add the pesto, pasta water, & lactose-free cream to a low-heat pan. Stir until the cream has fully incorporated.
