Chicken Shawarma

Active time: 15 minutes

Total time: 3 hours 15 minutes

Serves

Shawarma is usually slowly cooked on a rotisserie and shaved off in thin slices into a pita. Our version gives you equally tender and even juicier chicken that's simple to make at home. Use the chicken in a salad, on a pita, or over rice. However you serve it, you're going to love it.

Ingredients

    For the chicken

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, yellow, diced

    • For the yogurt sauce

  • 1/2 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Ground kosher salt
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes

    • For serving

  • Pitas, warmed
  • Chopped romaine
  • Halved cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber, thinly sliced or julienned

Directions

    For chicken

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together oil, lemon juice, garlic, and seasonings. Add chicken and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours and up to overnight.
  2. Preheat oven to 425Â° and grease a large baking sheet with cooking spray.
  3. Add onion to marinade and toss to coat. Remove chicken and onion from marinade and place on prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake until chicken is golden and cooked through, 30 minutes. Let chicken rest on cutting board for 5 minutes, then thinly slice.

    5. For yogurt sauce

  5. Blend in a food processor until light and fluffy. Store up to 2 weeks.

    Serving suggestions

  • Stuff into pita bread with romaine salad mixture. Optionally, you can quarter cherry tomatoes & slice cucumber and place those in the wrap with it. Drizzle the yogurt sauce over the salad.
