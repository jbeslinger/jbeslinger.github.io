Slow Cooker Chicken Cacciatore

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 6 hours

Serves

Italian classic, "hunter's chicken". Full of many feel-good veggies and protein, sure to spice up whatever pasta you top with.

Ingredients

    For the sauce

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs, bone-in, skin-on
  • 2 peppers, red bell
  • 8 ounces baby Bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 3/4 cups dry white wine
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/3 cup capers

    • For serving

  • 1 cup linguine, cooked

Directions

  1. Season chicken on both sides with salt and pepper and place in the slow cooker. Add peppers, mushrooms, garlic, tomatoes, wine, and broth, then season with oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken measures 160 internally. (Watch out for weird temps with the bone.)
  3. Remove the chicken from the slow-cooker and stir capers into the remaining sauce. Serve chicken over cooked pasta with sauce.
