Tom Yum

Active time: 45 minutes

Total time: 45 minutes

Serves

Tom Yum is a simple, but flavorful, soup made with fresh lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, fish sauce, chiles, and proteinâ€¦ Usually shrimp. Great for your immune system!

Ingredients

    For the soup

  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 2 peppers, thai red chili, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon galangal, minced (can sub w/ ginger)
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 5 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tomato, large, diced
  • 5 leaves, lime
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 6 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup lime juice (1 lime)
  • 1 tablespoon agave

    • Pick and mix these ingredients to your liking

  • Ramen noodles
  • Extra firm tofu, cooked
  • Shrimp, cooked
  • Chicken, cooked and in strips

Directions

  1. Trim off the top third and very bottom of the lemongrass stalks. Using the side of a knife, press down across the stalks to bruise the lemongrass and release the oils. Chop each stalk into 4 pieces and set aside.
  2. Add the chili peppers, garlic, and galangal to a mortar and pestle and grind into a paste. Set aside.
  3. Melt the coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat. Once the oil begins to shimmer, add the lemongrass and cook for 3-4 minutes, until fragrant. Add the chili pepper paste and cook for another 3-4 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Add the vegetable broth, tomato, lime leaves, coconut milk, fish sauce, and soy sauce and stir to combine. Bring to a boil. Cover, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes, until broth has reduced slightly.
  5. Add the mushrooms & whatever pix/mix ingredients you decided on and stir to combine. Cook for 5 minutes, until the mushrooms are tender.
  6. Add the lime juice and agave and stir to combine. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
