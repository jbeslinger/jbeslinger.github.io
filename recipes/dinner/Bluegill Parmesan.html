Bluegill Parmesan

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 40 minutes

Serves

Just started fishing for the first time in 15 years and don't know what to do with fish when you catch them? Why not parm them bluegills?

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons sprigs of parsley, minced
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pound bluegill, crappie, or perch

Directions

  1. Place butter in a shallow bowl. In another shallow bowl, combine the bread crumbs, cheese and seasonings.
  2. Dip fish in butter, then coat with crumb mixture.
  3. Place in a greased 15x10x1-in. baking pan. Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° for 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
