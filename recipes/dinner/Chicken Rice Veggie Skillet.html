Chicken, Rice, and Veggie Skillet

Active time: 15 minutes

Total time: 45 minutes

Serves

A skillet of fried rice, vegetables, and chicken breast. Very easy to make, just make sure to precook the necessary rice. Cooked rice : Raw rice, 3:1

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces olive oil
  • 1 pound chicken breast, diced
  • 1 onion, yellow diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, red medium diced
  • 1 head broccoli, florets
  • 3 cups rice, white cooked

    • For garnish

  • 3 onions, green sliced

Directions

  1. To a large skillet, add the onion and garlic to olive oil. Cook until translucent, 5 minutes.
  2. Add red bell peppers and chicken, cook for 5 minutes or until the chicken is cooked all the way.
  3. Add rice, broccoli, and water. Then season with paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook covered until the broccoli is tender.
  4. Serve and sprinkly with green onions.
