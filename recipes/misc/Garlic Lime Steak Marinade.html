Garlic Lime Steak Marinade

Active time: 5 minutes

Total time: 2 hours

Serves

Grabbed a tough cut of steak by mistake? Or on purpose because you're a cheap bastard? No worries, the lime juice and garlic in this marinade will break down the tough proteins in the steak & imbue a fantastic flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound steak, cut 1/2 inch thick

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients (except the steak, dummy) into a food processor or blender. Process until smooth paste.
  2. In a zip-loc bag, combine half the marinade with the steaks, making sure the meat is well coated. Let sit in fridge for 30 minutes. Set remaining marinade aside.
  3. Grill or (nonstick) pan fry steaks over high heat, 2-3 minutes per side. basting if necessary with remaining marinade. Remove to a serving dish, and brush with remaining marinade.
