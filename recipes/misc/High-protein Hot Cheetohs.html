High-Protein Flamin' Hot Cheetos

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 20 minutes

Serves

Not nearly as artery-cloggin' good as normal cheetohs, but deliciously fiery in their own way. And jam-packed with protein.

Ingredients

  • 6 servings liquid egg whites, room temp
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cream of Tartar
  • 5 drops red food coloring
  • 36 grams cheddar cheese powder
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoons garlic powder

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and line a baking sheet with foil and cooking spray.
  2. Using a hand mixer, mix the egg whites and cream of tartar on high for a few minutes or until your whites form stiff peaks.
  3. Add your red food coloring and mix a few more seconds until uniform.
  4. Line a large bowl with your cheese powder and spices.
  5. Using a piping bag, create long, red, Cheeto shaped egg whites onto your cheese mixture. The thinner, the crispier. You can make puffs by making them larger, just be wary of burning before the inside is fully cooked.
  6. Once your Cheetos are in the cheese, spoon the spices on top to fully spice each Cheeto.
  7. Place the finished Cheetos onto your baking sheet. If they're having trouble sticking together, your egg whites weren't stiff enough.
  8. Bake your Cheetos for 10-12 minutes. Spraying a tiny bit of cooking spray on top before baking will reducing the scorching of spices.
