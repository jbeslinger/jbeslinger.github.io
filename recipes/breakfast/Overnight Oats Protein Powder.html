Protein Overnight Oats

Active time: 5 minutes

Total time: 5 minutes

Serves

With protein overnight oats you can have your oatmeal and get your protein in! Each serving has over 20 grams of protein!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Almond butter (to taste)

Directions

  1. Simply combine everything in a container and add a pinch of salt. Then let sit overnight.
