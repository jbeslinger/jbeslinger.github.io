Maple Cinnamon Oatmeal Breakfast Bars

Active time: 15 minutes

Total time: 60 minutes

Servings: bars

Maple-cinnamon oatmeal bars are naturally sweet and gluten-free. Enjoy as a healthy snack or an easy, on-the-go breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups oats, steel-cut
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk (or sub, like almond/soy milk)
  • 1/2 cup banana, mashed
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup pecans, roughly chopped

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 and spray an 8"x8" baking pan with nonstick spray; set aside.
  2. Add the 2/3 of the oats to a blender until the oats have turned to flour.
  3. Add milk, banana, maple syrup, coconut oil, egg, and vanilla to a large bowl and whisk to combine. Add remaining 1/3 of the oats, oat flour, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt. Stir to combine & fold in the chopped pecans.
  4. Pour batter into the baking pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the edges brown and the center sets. Check on the bars at the 25 minute mark: if the edges are browning too fast, place foil on top of the pan.
  5. Let cool before slicing and storing. You can wrap the bars up in plastic wrap individually for ease.
