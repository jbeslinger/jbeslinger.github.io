Cereal and Fruit

Active time: 5 minutes

Total time: 5 minutes

Serves

A simple breakfast meal made of a bowl of cereal, a piece of fruit, and two slices of buttered toast. Good repleacement options for butter spread are margarine, olive oil, avocado, and cottage cheese.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces Cheerios
  • 1 cup almond/soy milk
  • 1 apple or banana
  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter (or butter substitute)
