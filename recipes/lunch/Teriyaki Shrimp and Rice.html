Rice Cooker Teriyaki Shrimp and Rice

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 40 minutes

Serves

By adding a few extra ingredients to your rice cooker, you can cook an entire meal at once. Super easy, healthy alternative to take out.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound raw shrimp, medium
  • 1 onion, small yellow
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 1/2 cups rice, jasmine or rice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh grated ginger
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar

    • For garnish

  • Green onions, slivered
  • Sriracha

Directions

  1. If the shrimp is frozen, place it in a colander and run cool water over it until thawed. (Only a few minutes.)
  2. Finely dice onion and place into the bottom of the cooker along with frozen peas. Then add rice, garlic, and ginger. Stir to combine.
  3. Add the shrimp on top of the rice mixture and top with water. Turn the rice cooker on.
  4. Once the rice cooker finishes its cooking cycle, let it rest unplugged for 10 minutes. While waiting, stir in soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, and brown sugar.
  5. Serve hot with green onions and sriracha.
