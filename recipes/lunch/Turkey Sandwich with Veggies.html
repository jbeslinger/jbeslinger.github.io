Turkey Sandwich and Vegetables

Active time: 5 minutes

Total time: 5 minutes

Serves

A basic lunch plan made of a turkey sandwich, vegetables, nuts, and a fruit. It covers all of the dietary pyramid. Can be made way in advance.

Ingredients

    For the sandwich

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 4 ounces lean turkey, sliced
  • 1 slice swiss, or hard cheese

    • To go with

  • 4 ounces baby carrots
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 1 cup apple sauce, unsweetened
  • 2 ounces mixed nuts, salted
