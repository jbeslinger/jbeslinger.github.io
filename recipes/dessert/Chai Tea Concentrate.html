Chai Tea Concentrate

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 10 minutes

Serves

Make your own chai concentrate at home with fresh herbs and spices for more flavor than you've ever experienced!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 4 bags black tea
  • 4 sticks cinnamon, small
  • 1 inch fresh ginger
  • 10 cloves, whole
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 5 pods cardamom

Directions

  1. Pour the water into a cooking pot. Add the cinnamon sticks, tea bags, whole cloves and ginger (peel and slice the fresh ginger).
  2. Slighty crush the cardamom pods and add to the pot with the rest of the spices
  3. Bring the pot up to a boil, place a lid on top, reduce the heat and simmer for five minutes to make sure it is brewed strong.
  4. Remove the pot from the heat and carefully strain out the tea bags and spices (Place a colander over a bowl and pour it in). Using whole spices not only adds more flavor but makes straining them out much much easier.
  5. While the spiced tea is still hot, stir in the sugar and vanilla. Store the chai concentrate in the refrigerator until you are ready to use. (Up to 1 week)
  6. To make a drink, combine one part chai concentrate with one part milk. Heat and serve!
