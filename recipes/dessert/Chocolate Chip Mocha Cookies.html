Chocolate Chip Mocha Cookies

Active time: 15 minutes

Total time: 25 minutes

Makes cookies

Deliciously soft, thick, and chewy chocolate cookies infused with coffee and studded with chocolate chunks. No chilling required!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg, large
  • 4 tablespoons coffee grounds
  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4, and line a baking tray with parchment paper, or a silicone mat. Set aside.
  2. Whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, cornflour, salt, and half the ground coffee. Set aside.
  3. Whisk together the butter and sugars until combined. Add the egg, and mix until combined.
  4. Mix the remaining coffee grounds in the hot water, then add to the wet ingredients, and mix until combined.
  5. Add the dry ingredients, and mix until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Roll the dough into 1.5oz balls & place on the prepared baking tray.
  7. Bake for 10 - 12 minutes. Do not over-bake. Allow to cool on the baking tray for 5 - 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
