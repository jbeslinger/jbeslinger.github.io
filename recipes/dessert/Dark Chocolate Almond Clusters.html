Dark Chocolate Almond Clusters

Active time: 5 minutes

Total time: 5 minutes

Serves

Easy to make, quick healthy dessert made with two ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup lightly salted almonds

    • For garnish

  • Sprinkle of kosher salt

Directions

  1. Melt chocolate in the microwave for 30 seconds until fully melted.
  2. Add almonds to melted chocolate.
  3. Portion the clusters into 1 tablespoon mounds on wax paper.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
