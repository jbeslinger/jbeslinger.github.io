Ambrosia Salad

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 70 minutes

Serves

Ambrosia salad is a staple side dish that's been around for years. Easy to make, crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup heavy cream, chilled
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 4 cups mini marshmallows, plain or fruity
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded coconut, sweetened
  • 11 ounces mandarin oranges, drained
  • 3/4 cup maraschino cherries, stems removed
  • 1 cup pineapple tidbits, drained

Directions

  1. In a medium sized bowl, combine the cream, sugar, and vanilla extract. Use an electric mixer on high speed until stiff peaks have formed. Like Cool Whip.
  2. Add sour cream and stir until combied.
  3. In a separate, large bowl, combine marshmallows, coconut, oranges, cherries, and pineapple. Stir.
  4. Pour the cream mixture over ingredients and stir until well combined.
  5. Cover and refrigerate at least one hour before serving.
