Black Tea And Peach Creamsicles

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 10 minutes

Makes popsicles

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups boiling water
  • 2 bags Pure Leaf Iced Black Tea with Peach Flavor
  • 1 peach, medium pitted and roughly cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons honey

Directions

  1. In a large liquid measuring cup, combine 1 1/2 cups boiling water and 2 Pure Leaf Iced Black Tea with Peach Flavor pyramid tea bags. Steep for 15 minutes, before discarding the tea bags. Carefully pour around 2 ounces of the tea into 6 popsicle molds. Drop a few peach chunks into each popsicle mold and transfer to the freezer to freeze for at least 3 hours to set.
  2. Once the tea layer has frozen, make the cream base. In a medium bowl, whisk together 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup heavy cream, 3 tablespoons honey, and remaining peach chunks. Divide evenly between the 6 popsicle molds and transfer to the freezer to set for at least 5 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. To serve, unmold each popsicle by dipping quickly in a tall glass of boiling water. Serve and enjoy immediately.
