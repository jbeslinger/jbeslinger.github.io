Blueberry Soft Serve

Active time: 5 minutes

Total time: 5 minutes

Serves

Calories:

Per serving:

Who doesn’t love ice cream? This creamy treat is packed full of antioxidants, and the coconut oil provides incredible fuel and nutrition while helping lower cholesterol levels. It’s the perfect dessert for grown-ups and kids alike.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons basil leaves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 lime, zest of
  • 1/4 teaspoon alcohol-free, gluten-free pure vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons bee pollen, for garnish

Directions

  1. It’s best to make this recipe in two batches. Combine half of the blueberries, coconut milk, oil, basil, honey (if using), lime zest, and vanilla in a blender, and blend on high, using the tamper to push the berries down toward the blade.
  2. Process until the mixture is smooth, thick, and creamy, about 1 minute.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a large bowl and place the bowl in the freezer.
  4. Repeat with the other half of the ingredients (except the bee pollen) and add to the first batch.
  5. Keep the first batch in the freezer only while you’re making the second batch.
  6. Scoop the ice cream into 4 bowls. Sprinkle each portion with 1 tablespoon bee pollen. Serve immediately. It’s best to eat this ice cream right away. Freezing it for too long will make it icy.
