Quick Donuts

Active time: 5 minutes

Total time: 5 minutes

Serves

Possibly the easiest, quickest, cheapest way to make donuts ever.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable or canola oil for frying
  • 1 package Pillsbury Buttermilk biscuits (5ct)

    • Cinnamon sugar donuts

  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons cinnamon

    • Jelly-filled donuts

  • Jelly - grape or whatever jam/jelly you prefer
  • Squeeze bottle
  • Powdered sugar

    • Maple-bourbon glazed donuts

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons bourbon
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Pour about 1.5" of oil in a small pot & bring to 360Â°.
  2. If you're making cinnamon sugar donuts, combine the sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
  3. Remove biscuits from package and separate.
  4. If you want the maple-bourbon glaze: melt the butter in a saucepan and slowly mix in the other ingredients, maple syrup, bourbon, and powdered sugar.
  5. If you're making jelly-filled donuts, skip this step. To make regular donuts, put a 1/2" hole in the middle of the biscuit and gently place in the oil, making sure to flip until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Remove from oil and place on a paper towel/wire rack. Once the donut is no longer dripping, take this opportunity to briefly toss it in the cinnamon-sugar bowl on both sides or in the maple-bourbon glaze on one side. OR, poke a hole in the donut with the spout of the squeeze bottle and squeeze in the jelly, & top the donut with some powdered sugar.
