Homemade Chocolate Chips

Active time: 10 minutes

Total time: 30 minutes

Makes tray

When you're too lazy to go to the store and have leftover cocoa powder for some reason.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Arrange ingredients. And place cocoa powder and sieve it. Leave out the crumbs and set aside.
  2. Sieve the all purpose flour as well then mix the cocoa. Add sugar and salt and mix well.
  3. Add in milk (normal temp) and mix well until powder is dissolved. Place the pot on stove now and turn flame low. Keep stirring until thick consistency. Then add butter and vanilla essence and mix well. Until smooth. Turn flame off and let the chocolate cool.
  4. Arrange baking paper or butter paper into a pan. Pour or pipe the chocolate onto the surface.
  5. Place the pan in the freezer for 20 minutes & shatter the chocolate into chunks when it comes out using a hammer or the blunt end of a knife.
