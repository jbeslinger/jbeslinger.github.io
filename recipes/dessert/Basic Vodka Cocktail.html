Basic Vodka Cocktail

Active time: 5 minutes

Total time: 5 minutes

Makes drink

Generic vodka cocktail; you can put any kind of juice you're feeling in this & it'll be fantastic. It's also quite healthy; you could down one of these a day and fight heart disease.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka (1 part)
  • 4 1/2 ounces fruit juice, no sugar added (2 parts)
  • 1 1/2 ounces soda water (1 part)
  • 2 handfuls crushed ice
  • 1 wedge, citrus fruit

Directions

  1. Place vodka, juice, and half the ice into a drink shaker. Shake until combined.
  2. Fill a glass up with the other half of the ice and pour the cocktail over it. Then, add the soda water.
  3. Serve with a citrus wedge on the corner of the glass.
